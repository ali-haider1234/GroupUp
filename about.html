Ali Haider

I love building student-first tools that make collaboration feel simple and real.

GroupUp exists because students struggle to form FYP groups without a clear, organized system. It helps you connect with people who share your interests, so the team you build actually fits your idea. When the right people come together, creativity grows and the work gets stronger. I care about building tools that genuinely help students move forward, and seeing that impact makes me happy. This project is my way of giving back to my community and society.
